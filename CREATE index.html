Free Health Checkup Camp
Lions Club Ground · 9AM–2PM · Sat
HealthFreeSaturday
What you'll do: Help patients register at the desk, guide them through checkup stations (BP, blood sugar, eye, dental), assist doctors with basic tasks and crowd management. No medical background needed — a friendly attitude is all that's required.

What to bring: Comfortable footwear, water bottle, ID proof. Lunch provided by Lions Club.

Impact: Last camp served 340 patients in one day. Doctors from Apollo & Fortis volunteering on-site.

Mental Health Awareness Camp
Community Centre, Nikol · 10AM
Mental HealthFree
What you'll do: Assist trained counsellors in reaching daily wage workers. Help set up awareness booths, distribute pamphlets in Gujarati & Hindi, listen empathetically and guide people to counsellors. Sensitivity training provided on the day.

Why it matters: Daily wage workers have the highest rate of untreated anxiety in Gujarat. This camp aims to screen 200 individuals and refer 50 for free follow-up sessions.

Requirement: 18+, basic language skills in Gujarati/Hindi. Empathy is your superpower here.

Education Drives
16
Mar
Sunday Education Drive
Community Hall, Behrampura · 10AM–1PM
EducationFree
What you'll teach: Math (basic arithmetic, fractions) and English (reading, simple sentences) for children aged 8–14 from low-income families. Lesson plans are pre-prepared — you just need to explain them clearly and interact warmly with kids.

Who can volunteer: Any graduate or 12th-pass student. Teaching experience is a plus but not mandatory. We pair first-timers with experienced volunteers.

Impact so far: 890 kids taught across 48 sessions. 12 children secured school scholarships this year through this programme.

Digital Literacy Workshop
Govt School, Odhav · 11AM
DigitalFree
What you'll teach: How to use UPI apps (PhonePe, GPay) for safe payments, how to access government portals (Digilocker, Aadhaar, ration card), and how to avoid online scams. Target audience: elderly adults, 55–75 years.
